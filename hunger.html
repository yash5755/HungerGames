The Four Trials

150 Tributes Enter · 1 Victor Emerges

◆
I
Round 01 3-Phased Digital Trial
The Bloodbath
// 3-Phased Digital Trial //
120 ParticipantsMystery ImageArchitecture LeakMCQ Speed Quiz

Round 1 runs as a 3-phased digital trial: Mystery Image (Multiplier 1), Architecture Leak (Multiplier 2), and MCQ Speed Quiz. The final output is the Top 60 survivors.

  • Phase 1 — Mystery Image challenge using blurred, zoomed, or partial visuals
  • Phase 2 — Architecture Leak challenge using system diagrams and logic questions
  • Phase 3 — MCQ Speed Quiz (20 to 30 questions, 10 to 15 seconds each)
  • Final Score = (MCQ Score) x (Image Multiplier) x (Architecture Multiplier)
  • Execution model: single Google Form with 3 sections, then sheet-based Top 60 ranking
⚠ Outcome: Top 60 scorers advance · Bottom 60 eliminated
II
Round 02 Digital Logical Labyrinth
The Tracker Jacker Trail
// Digital Logical Labyrinth //
60 ParticipantsGoogle FormsBranching LogicTop 20 Advance

Sixty participants, including any revived wild tributes, compete in a branching Google Form maze that simulates a data packet’s journey through a network.

  • Three gate checkpoints — Section 1, Section 2, and Section 3 must be completed in sequence
  • Dead End Riddle traps send wrong answers back and cost valuable time
  • Branching logic simulates network path decisions
  • The first 20 participants to correctly complete and submit advance
⚠ Outcome: First 20 advance to Round 3
III
Round 03 Lightning Ideathon
The Mockingjay's Blueprint
// Lightning Ideathon //
20 Semifinalists5 ProblemsArchitecture90 Sec Pitch

Twenty semifinalists compete in a mini design and architecture challenge built around five problem statements and delivered in three stages.

  • Stage 1 — Strategy and Selection (5 minutes): choose a problem statement or use-case
  • Stage 2 — Design the Solution (30 minutes): build a digital system architecture that addresses scalability, security, and logic
  • Stage 3 — The Pitch (90 seconds): deliver a short verbal pitch to the judges
  • Judging criteria: scalability, security, and logical structure of the architecture
⚠ Outcome: Top 5 Finalists Selected
IV
Round 04 · The Finale Live On Stage · Full Audience
The Victor's Podium
// The Final Showdown · Spotlighted Stage //
Final 5Live AudienceStump the ArchitectRapid Fire

The final five participants compete on stage in front of the full audience. Judges use rapid-fire technical questions to test depth of knowledge and quick thinking.

  • Stump the Architect — rapid-fire technical questions test depth and quick thinking
  • The winner is judged on clarity, technical depth, and handling surprise questions
  • Live audience participation and judge evaluation happen in real time
  • Only one Victor is crowned and receives the AWS Winner's Badge
👑 Outcome: 1 Victor Crowned · AWS Winner's Badge
