The Arena

The Four Trials

150 Tributes Enter · 1 Victor Emerges

◆
I
Round 01 Digital Speed Trial
The Bloodbath
// Digital Speed Trial //
120 Tributes45 MinutesMCQ QuizSpeed + Accuracy

All 120 Tributes clash simultaneously in a 3-phased digital trial. Two Multiplier Challenges precede a rapid-fire MCQ quiz. Every second counts — speed and accuracy are both survival weapons.

  • Phase 1 & 2 — Multiplier Challenges: Mystery Image & Architecture Leak
  • Phase 3 — 10 rapid-fire MCQ questions across 45 minutes
  • Topics: General Tech · Pattern Recognition · Logic · Geek Culture · Aptitude
  • Booster questions award extra survival score multipliers
⚠ Outcome: Top 60 Survivors Advance
II
Round 02 Digital Logic Labyrinth
The Tracker Jacker Trial
// Digital Logic Labyrinth //
60 SurvivorsGoogle FormsBranching LogicFirst 20 Submit

60 survivors navigate a Google Form maze with branching logic — simulating a data packet travelling a network. Wrong turns loop you back. Dead ends cost precious time.

  • The Data Packet's Journey — navigate paths like a network packet
  • 3 gate checkpoints (Section 1 → Section 2 → Section 3) in sequence
  • Dead End Riddle traps loop participants back and cost time
  • First 20 to correctly complete and submit the full form advance
⚠ Outcome: Top 20 Semifinalists Advance
III
Round 03 Lightning Ideathon
The Mockingjay's Blueprint
// Lightning Ideathon //
20 ArchitectsScalabilitySecurityLogic

20 Semifinalists design cloud-native Survival Blueprints for complex architectural scenarios. Three intense stages test strategy, design depth, and the power of your pitch.

  • Stage 1 — Strategy & Selection (5 min): Choose your problem statement or use-case
  • Stage 2 — Design the Solution (30 min): Build a system architecture addressing scalability, security & logic
  • Stage 3 — The Pitch (90 sec): Verbal defense to the judges panel
  • Judged on: Scalability · Security · Logical Architecture Structure
⚠ Outcome: Top 5 Finalists Selected
IV
Round 04 · The Finale Live On Stage · Full Audience
The Victor's Podium
// The Final Showdown · Spotlighted Stage //
5 FinalistsLive JudgingRapid FireSingle Podium

The Final Five take the stage in front of the full audience. There is no shield. No preparation time. Only clarity, depth, and composure can crown a Victor.

  • 5 finalists compete simultaneously on the spotlighted main stage
  • Stump the Architect — rapid-fire technical questions on depth & quick thinking
  • Live audience and judge evaluation in real time
  • Winner evaluated on clarity, technical depth, and handling surprise questions
👑 Outcome: 1 Victor Crowned · AWS Winner's Badge
